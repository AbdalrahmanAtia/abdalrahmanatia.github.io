Hi! I am a Software Engineering graduate and aspiring cinema director from Egypt, currently preparing for a long-term European Solidarity Corps volunteering project in Brno, Czech Republic (Sep 2025 – Aug 2026). My interests lie at the intersection of robotics, manipulation, AI, and cinematic storytelling.

I have gained diverse international experience studying and working abroad, including internships in Singapore, study abroad in the USA, and research projects in Russia. I am passionate about robotics research, focusing on manipulation, sim2real transfer, foundation models for planning, and generalist robot systems.

I have received multiple academic awards such as the USAID Scholars Activity Award and participated in competitive programming contests (ACM ECPC). Currently, I am seeking funded Master's opportunities in Europe while preparing to volunteer and contribute to the robotics and AI community globally.
  Research

I am interested in building generalist agents that are capable of solving any task of interest. To that end, my work focuses on unlocking generalization in robot learning by enabling agents to efficiently explore in the real world, leveraging hierarchy to quickly solve new tasks, performing imitation learning at scale to train powerful generalist agents and integrating large-scale learning into hierarchical systems to enable broadly capable manipulation systems.
