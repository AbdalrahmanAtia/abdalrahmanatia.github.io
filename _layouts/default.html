Hi! I am a recent Software Engineering graduate from the Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence at Cairo University, Egypt. My undergraduate studies were fully funded through the USAID Scholars Activity—a competitive, merit-based scholarship managed by the American University in Cairo and USAID Egypt. As part of the program, I spent a semester as a visiting student at the University of Louisville in the United States, where I studied advanced topics in machine learning and embedded systems and engaged with international faculty and researchers.

I was a research intern at the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research (JINR) in Dubna, Russia, where I worked under the supervision of Dr. Slavomir Hnatic in the MLIT division on the software of the MPD/NICA project. Later, I joined the Singapore Institute of Manufacturing Technology (SIMTech) at A*STAR through the Singapore International Pre-Graduate Award (SIPGA), where I contributed to robotics research supervised by Dr. Haiyue Zhu with Elvin Toh. Earlier, I also interned at Halliburton and participated in the ACM ECPC (Egyptian Collegiate Programming Contest), gaining hands-on experience in algorithmic problem-solving and system design.

I am currently seeking a remote research assistant, internship, or full-time PhD opportunities starting Fall 2026.
